Meet Pete Conway, a passionate adventurer and excited new member of the London Calling Ocean rowing team. With over 40 years of rowing experience, Pete brings a wealth of knowledge and skill to the team. A lifelong explorer, Pete has spent decades sailing, surfing, and rowing across the globe, developing a deep appreciation for the power and rhythm of the ocean. Supported by his amazing wife and three awesome daughters, Pete is eager to contribute to the team’s mission and share his passion for adventure with his fellow rowers as they take on the challenging task of rowing across the Atlantic.

Pete has chosen to row for two incredible charities: The Boston House and Combined Jewish Philanthropies (CJP).

The Boston House provides a home away from home for children with cancer and their families. Imagine the comfort and support it offers during such a challenging time. By supporting Pete, you’re helping to ensure that these families have access to essential resources and a safe, welcoming environment.

CJP’s Center for Combating Antisemitism is working tirelessly to combat the rise of anti-Jewish hate in Greater Boston. Your support will help fund vital programs, education initiatives, and community outreach efforts. Together, we can create a more inclusive and tolerant society for all.

Join Pete’s journey and make a tangible difference. Your donation will help him reach his goal and support these two vital charities. Let’s show our support for Pete and his noble cause!

Pete and his wife, Lucy live in Topsfield, MA with their kids.
